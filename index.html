3D AnimationFurball (2025)

A 3D animation created in Blender using liquid simulation.

A site-specific 3D animation created for the group exhibition "Virzienmaiņas" ("Shifts"). The work presents a speculative scenario in which the Daugava briefly alters its course and enters the Riga Contemporary Art Space.

Using a digitally reconstructed model of the gallery and Blender's fluid simulation system, the work visualizes water rushing down the entrance stairs and gradually transforming the familiar interior into an unfamiliar landscape.

3D animācija, veidota Blender programmā, izmantojot šķidruma simulāciju.

Vietas specifiska 3D animācija, radīta grupu izstādei "Virzienmaiņas". Darbs piedāvā spekulatīvu scenāriju, kurā Daugava uz brīdi maina savu tecējumu un ieplūst Rīgas Laikmetīgās mākslas telpā.

The artist

Raimonds Krastiņš

I have been involved in art for as long as I can remember. Since the age of eight, I have attended art schools and extracurricular art programs, developing a foundation in both traditional and contemporary artistic practices.

In 2022, I graduated from the Janis Rozentāls Art School, and in 2026 I received a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Art at the Art Academy of Latvia. Today, I work primarily with Blender, creating 3D artworks and animations inspired by liminal spaces, overlooked architecture, and the atmosphere of everyday environments.

Through my work, I explore memories, places, and objects, creating art pieces that blur the boundaries between digital and physical space.
